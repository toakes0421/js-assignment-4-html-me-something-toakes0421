New Hampshire. Where it all started.

Not born but raised in a log cabin in the sleepy (and racially homogenous) college town of Durham, our hero began his journey much in the same way of Abraham Lincoln. Unfortunately, the similarities ended once he topped out just under 5'10", and found growing a serviceable beard might not be in the cards. And no, while he didn't become student body president either, he did graduate on time with decent grades and a few college credits to boot, all while finding the time to occasionally experiment with drugs. A mediocre lacrosse player, Trevor instead excelled at guitar, (going on to do nothing with these skills) and being editor in chief of the school paper (same story here too). With a bright but not too bright future ahead of him, our main man set off to the least relevant state in America, Delaware, to tackle university.

Delaware. The college years.

Now with a credibility boost thanks to the recent election of alumnus "Sleepy Joe", way back in 2014 the University of Delaware had some what of a different repution. Well known only for producing mediocre NFL quarterbacks like Joe Flacco and noticeably less than mediocre state governors like Chris Cristie, Trevor came into the start of his four year schooling journey with the opportunity to leave a less shitty stain. And did he? Not in the least, though he half-assed membership with Engineers Without Borders for a year and also figured out how to make a bong out of a potato. Oh, and got a decent story out of swimming in the president's pool. But don't let his 3.5 GPA and degree in Mechanical Engineering fool you; this sucker learned almost nothing useful to take into the working world, instead comning away with a massive rip in his soul regarding life's purpose, as well as a pretty hefty loan bill that couldn't be fully covered by extremely generous saving on behalf of his family. Booksmart, and stupid in just about every other way, our hero sheepishly made his way to the nearest large metropolis to his school in the hopes that he could cling to some acquaintance for social stability...

Philadelphia. Where we now meet our protaganist.

Why Philly? I guess you could say he had nowhere else to go.
